# Project-management-plan

A comprehensive, 74-page project management plan authored for CIT 263 (Project Management) at TMCC, Spring 2025. The document walks through the full lifecycle of a project from initiation to closure.

## Overview
This plan covers a complete, 74-page project management plan for "The Vault Warehouse," a fictional $36 million NIKE warehouse construction and technology integration project. Acting as Project Manager, I produced the full set of project management deliverables across all five process groups Initiating, Planning, Executing, Monitoring & Controlling, and Closing. The project covers the design, construction, technology integration (including a Warehouse Management System and security systems), testing, and launch of a 50,000 square foot distribution facility on a 19-month timeline. The plan demonstrates how a project manager defines scope, estimates costs, schedules work, manages stakeholders and risk, and tracks progress through real challenges including a simulated 10% mid-project budget cut that required a two-month schedule extension and active cost management to deliver on time and within budget. It demonstrates end-to-end planning, professional documentation, and structured thinking across every phase of a project.

## What's Inside
- Project scope and objectives
- Requirements gathering and analysis
- Scheduling and milestone planning
- Risk identification and management
- Resource and budget planning
- Execution and closure strategy

## Why It Matters
This deliverable shows the ability to plan, document, and communicate a complex project clearly and at length — skills that apply directly to IT and security roles, where documentation and structured planning are essential.
